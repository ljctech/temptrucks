Our Story:

Created by State Farm® teammember who discovered how competitive State Farm® commerical auto is.

I had a client call me up for a commerical insurance, at the end of the call it turned out I was saving him $3,000 dollars a year per truck he had 4 trucks.

That conversation easily turned into quotes for their personal policies and later on he referred his employees.

I was hooked, I wanted to reach out to more companies that had trucks, but how?
I tried the phone book and called companies I thought might have trucks, but I wasted alot of my time calling prospects who either did not have trucks or did not meet the underwriting guidlines of State Farm.

There was one place that had the information I needed. The Department Of Transportation; the S.A.F.E.R. site is not searchable in a way that would allow for creating a prescreened list.

I teamed up with another teammember in my office and we started work on a project that would enable people to search the DOT database.

This site allows you to create a custom search, for trucks you are most competitive.
